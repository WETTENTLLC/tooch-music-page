ARTIST BIO

Tooch Magooch is an emerging artist known for blending various musical styles into a unique sound that captivates audiences worldwide. With influences ranging from hip-hop to electronic and R&B, Tooch Magooch creates music that defies traditional genre boundaries.

Born and raised in a vibrant musical community, Tooch discovered their passion for music at an early age. Starting with piano lessons and progressing to digital production, they have developed a signature sound that combines organic instruments with cutting-edge electronic elements.

Their debut singles have garnered attention from music blogs and streaming platforms alike, with tracks reaching thousands of listeners across multiple countries. Each release showcases Tooch's ability to craft melodies that resonate with diverse audiences while maintaining artistic integrity.
