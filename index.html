About

Tooch MaGooch, an emerging artist from Sacramento, CA, is making significant waves in the music industry. Her unique sound, blending raw lyricism with captivating beats, has garnered widespread acclaim. Tooch's latest single, "PRESS," is rapidly climbing the charts, achieving notable success on both local and national radio stations. The track has secured a prominent position on the BDS radio charts and is currently in rotation on major stations, including KSFM 102.5, KWIN 97.7/98.5, and various other platforms, reflecting her growing influence and popularity.
Her journey began with the viral hit "Munyunn," a track that she now legally owns, further solidifying her independence and control over her creative work. This ownership is a testament to her business acumen and dedication to her craft. "Munyunn" and "1942" (featuring Rayven Justice) became city anthems, earning her features in The Source and establishing her as a formidable talent.
Tooch has collaborated with renowned artists such as Mozzy ("Mine") and DaBoii ("Lemme See Something Remix"), showcasing her versatility and respect within the rap community. Her dynamic performances and authentic storytelling resonate deeply with fans, promising a bright future for this rising star. With her distinct style and unwavering determination, Tooch MaGooch is poised to become a household name.
